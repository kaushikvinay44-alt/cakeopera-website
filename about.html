About Cakeopera

Cakeopera is a modern bakery brand based in Gurgaon, specialising in cheesecakes, rich chocolate cakes, truffle cakes, jar desserts and pastries – crafted fresh every single day.

Our Philosophy

No shortcuts, no compromise. We use premium ingredients, slow baking and carefully tested recipes to deliver desserts that are rich, balanced and memorable – not just sweet.

What We’re Known For

  • New York–style baked cheesecakes
  • Biscoff, Blueberry & Nutella flavours
  • Chocolate & truffle celebration cakes
  • Perfectly portioned jar desserts

Perfect For

  • Birthdays & anniversaries
  • Office celebrations
  • Small house parties
  • Late-night dessert cravings
